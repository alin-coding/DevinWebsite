ABOUT ME

Devin Downing grew up in Temecula, California. He spent his childhood reading books on the beach and bashing bones at the skate park. Currently, Devin lives in Portland, Oregon with his wife and newborn son. Apart from crafting complex stories, he enjoys hiking, ice-hockey, and writing about himself in the third person (yep, it's me! Hi guys!). Today, he's in his final year of medical school, with plans to specialize in psychiatry. Despite a full schedule, Devin hasn't given up on writing. In addition to The Adamic Trilogy, he has several more projects that he can't wait to share with the world.
